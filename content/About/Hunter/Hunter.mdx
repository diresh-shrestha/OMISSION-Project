Hunter Wolff is a multi-disciplinary artist and designer from Illinois. In May, he will graduate from New York University,
receiving a B.S. in Media, Culture, and Communication and a minor in Studio Art. In his own practice,
Hunter creates art projects that cover contemporary topics of social intimacy, futurism, and the anthropocene.
