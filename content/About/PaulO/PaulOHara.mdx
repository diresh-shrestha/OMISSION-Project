Paul is a Michigan based artist and multi-disciplinary designer.
He is a recent graduate of the College for Creative Studies in Detroit
with a BFA in Industrial Design and a Minor in Illustration.
When he is not working on the Omission Project you'll find him
designing logos, working on illustrating children's books,
creating concept artwork, and playing around with his 3d printer.
